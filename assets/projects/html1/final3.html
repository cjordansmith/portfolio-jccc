content is king

quality

More than anything else, are you producing quality content? Quality content can drive traffic towards your webpage on its own. If you are creating content that makes users visit your page more often, stay longer, and feel that their experience was better than a competitor, they can help increase traffic with other users.

research

Perhaps the most important SEO factor after creating good content is good keyword research. There are a variety of tools that allow you to discover the specific ways that people may be searching for your content.

keywords

You want to create content using those keywords, the actual search terms people are using, so you can produce content that effectively “answers” that query. The more keywords added to your page, the better. It's a good idea to add mis-spellings and related words as well. This practice will make sure the user can find what they are looking for if they make a mistake.

freshness

Search engines love new content. That’s usually what we mean when we say ‘fresh’. So you can’t update your pages every day thinking that will make them ‘fresh’ and more likely to rank. Nor can you just add new pages constantly, just for the sake of having new pages, and think that gives you a freshness boost.

answers

Search engines are increasing trying to show direct answers within their search results. Questions like “why is the sky blue” or “how old is Barack Obama” might give you the answer without needing to click to a web page. Where do search engines get these answers? Sometimes, they license them, such as with menus or music lyrics. Other times, they draw them directly off web pages, providing a link back in the form of a credit.
